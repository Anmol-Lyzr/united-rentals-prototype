## 3. Rental Rates & Fee Structure

| Tier | Period | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Daily** | 1 day | Based on 8-hour single-shift usage |
| **Weekly** | 7 days | Based on 40 hours/week |
| **Monthly** | 28 days | Based on 160 hours/4-week period |

- **Rates are location-dependent** — always enter ZIP code in catalog for exact pricing
- Online/app orders receive **exclusive discounted rates** vs. in-store or phone
- Corporate accounts may have **pre-negotiated custom pricing agreements**
- Rental charges **accrue on Saturdays, Sundays, and Holidays**

### 3.3 Mandatory Fees (Always Added to Invoice)

| Fee | Description |
|---|---|
| **Delivery & Pickup Service Charge** | Disclosed on Rental & Service Agreement; varies by equipment size and distance |
| **Environmental Service Charge** | Applies to certain rentals; on work orders: 2% of service fees, max $99; not government-mandated |
| **State & Local Taxes** | Not included in base rental rate |
| **Transportation Surcharges** | For oversized or heavy equipment requiring special transport |

### 3.4 Conditional Fees (Avoidable by Customer)

| Fee | Trigger | How to Avoid |
|---|---|---|
| **Refueling Service Charge** | Equipment returned with less than a full fuel tank | Return equipment with full tank |
| **Late Payment Fee** | Balances >30 days past due | 2% per month (24% p.a.) — pay on time |
| **Credit Card Surcharge** | Paying with CC on a charge account (where permitted by law) | 2% surcharge; use ACH/check if available |
| **Tolling Service Charge** | Using toll-by-plate system with a licensed vehicle | Toll amount + $3.30 admin fee; avoid by using own transponder or avoiding toll roads |
| **Additional Mileage Charge** | Exceeding included mileage on licensed over-the-road vehicles | Track mileage per rental agreement |
| **Excess Cleaning / Restoration** | Returning equipment with alterations, dirty tanks, or modified configuration | Return equipment clean and in original configuration |

### 3.5 Off-Rent & Grace Period Rules *(Critical for Billing Disputes)*

- **Billing STOPS when the customer calls to put equipment "off rent"** — NOT when it is physically picked up.
- Customer receives an **Off-Rent Confirmation Number** on the call — always note this.
- Equipment may sit on jobsite for days or weeks after off-rent call with **zero additional charge**.
- If customer continues using equipment after off-rent call, accrued hours may be charged.
- **Grace period = time between off-rent call and actual pickup** (free to customer).
- ⚠️ **#1 dispute cause:** Customer claims verbal "grace period" promised extended billing — always obtain the confirmation number in writing.
- If UR fails to pick up promptly after a confirmed off-rent call, no additional charges should apply.

### 3.6 Cost Savings Guidance for Support Staff to Share

- Book online or via app for exclusive lower rates
- Weekly rate beats 4+ daily rentals; monthly beats ~18+ daily rentals
- Total Control® users save **15–35% annually** through consumption management
- Return equipment with full fuel tank to avoid refueling surcharge
- Call off-rent immediately when equipment is no longer needed
- Use Total Control 3-day idle alerts to catch underutilized equipment early
- Requesting substitutions can lower costs (e.g., 19-ft lift vs. 20-ft lift at lower rate)

## 8. Rental Policies & Protection

### 8.1 Rental Protection Plan (RPP)

- **NOT insurance** — it is a damage waiver product
- **Mandatory** unless customer presents proof of qualifying property insurance (per Section 22 of US Rental Service Terms)
- Can be declined only with valid proof of insurance

| Scenario | Customer Liability with RPP |
|---|---|
| Standard equipment damage/theft | Lesser of: 10% replacement value, 10% repair cost, or $500 (+tax) |
| Passenger vehicle damage/theft | Amounts above first $7,500 per rental period |
| Passenger vehicle RPP rate | 25% of rental charges |

**Exclusions:** Tires (most states), misuse, unauthorized operator damage, willful misconduct

**To file a claim:** Contact local store or call 844-873-4948 (24/7)

### 8.2 Customer Insurance Requirements (if opting out of RPP)

- Must show proof of adequate liability, physical damage, and casualty insurance
- Policy must include **waiver of subrogation** against United Rentals
- Insurer must provide **90 days' notice** before policy cancellation
- Customer is solely responsible for deductibles and self-insured retentions

### 8.3 Operator Licensing Requirements

| Equipment | Requirement |
|---|---|
| Aerial lifts (boom, scissor) | OSHA operator certification mandatory |
| Large trucks & trailers | CDL Class B or C per state law |
| Forklifts | OSHA-compliant forklift certification |
| All equipment | Unauthorized operator use = violation of rental terms & may void RPP |

### 8.4 Rental Agreement Key Terms

- Rental period: "Rental Out" date to "Scheduled In" date per agreement
- Charges accrue 7 days/week including holidays
- Customer responsible for safekeeping from delivery until UR retrieval
- United reserves right to change prices; effective upon website posting or email notice
- Governing law: state in which the store location is located
- Class action waiver: all claims must be pursued individually
- Late payment: 2% per month (24% per annum) on balances >30 days

## 12. United Guard™ Warranty

### 12.1 What It Is
- A **service contract** (NOT a manufacturer's warranty or insurance)
- Covers components of applicable new and used equipment purchased from United Rentals
- Covers components not designed to be replaced during product lifetime (motors, transmissions, etc.)
- Available to original **commercial purchasers only**

### 12.2 Eligibility & Terms

| Condition | Requirement |
|---|---|
| Equipment value | Must be over $2,500 |
| Equipment age | Must be 9 years old or newer |
| Vehicles | NOT eligible for United Guard |
| Used equipment condition | Must be in "rental ready" condition at time of sale |
| Enrollment window | Must purchase **within 72 hours** of equipment purchase |
| Renewal | Cannot be renewed once the term expires |
| Coverage terms | Choose 3, 6, or 12 months |

### 12.3 Claims & Cancellation
- To file a claim: contact your local United Rentals store
- If equipment cannot be repaired or cost exceeds original purchase price, UR replaces with comparable equipment (warranty term then ends)
- Cancellation after 30 days: pro-rata refund minus $25 cancellation fee or 10% of pro-rata amount (whichever is less), minus cost of any claims paid
- Replacement parts may include new, remanufactured, or non-OEM parts meeting quality standards

## 15. Common Customer Issues & Resolution Paths

This section is a quick-reference guide for support staff.

### 15.1 Billing Disputes

| Issue | Steps to Resolve |
|---|---|
| **Overcharged after return** | 1. Ask for Off-Rent Confirmation Number. 2. Verify "Rental Out" and "Scheduled In" dates on rental agreement. 3. Check if equipment was used after off-rent call. 4. Escalate to Account Services: 1-833-459-9575 if discrepancy confirmed. |
| **Unexpected fees on invoice** | 1. Identify fee type (Environmental, Refueling, Delivery, Tolling, Mileage). 2. Cross-reference against rental agreement disclosures. 3. Refueling charge: confirm if equipment was returned full. 4. Escalate to Account Services if fee not disclosed on agreement. |
| **Late payment fee dispute** | 1. Check payment date vs. invoice due date. 2. Late fee = 2%/month (24% p.a.) after 30 days — this is contractual. 3. Goodwill waiver requests escalate to branch manager. |
| **PO mismatch on invoice** | 1. Check PO tab in Total Control for current vs. original PO value. 2. Rental extension beyond PO value requires PO update. 3. Customer's accounting team must update PO before invoice processing. |
| **RPP damage charge dispute** | 1. Confirm RPP was purchased (on rental agreement). 2. Review RPP limits: 10% replacement value, 10% repair cost, or $500 (whichever is lesser). 3. If damage is excluded (tire, misuse), explain exclusions. 4. Escalate to branch manager for inspection report. |

### 15.2 Delivery & Equipment Issues

| Issue | Steps to Resolve |
|---|---|
| **Equipment not delivered on time** | 1. Check order status in mobile app (Preparing/En Route/Delivered). 2. Verify 72-hour lead time was given. 3. Call 1-833-459-9575 (Account Services) for escalation. 4. Branch will procure from network if needed. |
| **Wrong equipment delivered** | 1. Do NOT use equipment if wrong. 2. Call 844-873-4948 immediately. 3. Document with photos. 4. UR will dispatch correct equipment or make it right. |
| **Equipment breakdown on jobsite** | 1. Submit service request via mobile app with photos. 2. Or call 844-873-4948 (24/7). 3. Track tech ETA in app. 4. For critical breakdown: UR dispatches on-site mechanic. |
| **Equipment late pickup after off-rent** | 1. Get Off-Rent Confirmation Number if not already obtained. 2. No charges accrue after confirmation number is issued. 3. Call 1-833-459-9575 to expedite pickup. |
| **Availability issue / item out of stock** | 1. Check substitution suggestions in online catalog. 2. Adjust dates or location parameters. 3. Call branch or 844-873-4948 — rep will source from network. |

### 15.3 Account & Digital Tool Issues

| Issue | Steps to Resolve |
|---|---|
| **Can't log in to Total Control** | 1. Use same email/password as main UR account. 2. Check if Web PIN was set up. 3. Call 844-474-8520 or email totalcontrol@ur.com. |
