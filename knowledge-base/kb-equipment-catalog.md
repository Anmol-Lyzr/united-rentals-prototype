## 1. Aerial Work Platforms

United Rentals maintains the largest aerial fleet in North America. All aerial equipment requires the operator to hold a valid aerial work platform certification (ANSI A92 / CSA B354 compliant training). UR offers on-site and online operator training through United Academy.

**Key Rental Notes:**
- Electric slab scissors are non-marking tires — suitable for finished floors.
- Rough terrain models require firm, level ground rated for machine weight.
- All scissor lifts must be used with harness and lanyard when platform height exceeds 6 ft (OSHA 1926.453).

### 1.4 Aerial Attachments

- **Pipe Cradles** — Fits most boom lifts 45 ft+. Supports up to 1,000 lb of pipe/conduit alongside personnel.
- **Glazier / Panel Kits** — Extends platform beyond standard guardrails for glass installation. Available for articulating booms 45–80 ft.
- **Fall Arrest Anchor Systems** — SRL (self-retracting lifeline) kits, full-body harnesses (S/M/L/XL), and energy-absorbing lanyards. Available for rental or purchase.
- **Material Handling Attachments** — Platform-mounted winches (300 lb capacity) for tools and small materials.
- **Welding Lead Trays** — Insulated trays for safe routing of welding leads in aerial platforms.
